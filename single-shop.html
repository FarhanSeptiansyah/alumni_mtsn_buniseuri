The Breitling Datora series has gone through a style evolution from its early days of having a calendar dial configuration in the 1940s; but by the 1970s, it had become a stylish and funky chronograph design synonymous with that decade. This example in particular retains that 1970s style with a large tonneau case by famous case-maker Ervin Piquerez S.A. (EPSA). What’s particularly interesting is that it is in black PVD and does not feature the eye-catching orange hands that were often used for this series.
